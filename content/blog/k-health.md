## Executive Summary

K Health is an AI-driven digital healthcare company that provides virtual primary care services through a clinical AI platform. Founded in 2016 in Israel and headquartered in New York, the company combines artificial intelligence with telemedicine to deliver personalized, affordable healthcare accessible 24/7. K Health has raised approximately $438 million in total funding and achieved unicorn status with a $1.4 billion valuation in January 2021, though its valuation decreased to $900 million in its most recent 2024 funding round. The company partners with major U.S. health systems including Cedars-Sinai, Hackensack Meridian Health, Hartford HealthCare, and Northwell Health to provide integrated virtual care.

### Mission & Vision

**Mission**: "To use the power of shared knowledge to get everyone access to better, affordable healthcare - whenever, wherever they need it." [2021-04-14](https://www.blackstone.com/news/press/k-health-blackstone-growth-anthem-to-partner-on-technology-joint-venture-to-advance-more-affordable-high-quality-healthcare/)

**Vision**: To give everyone access to high-quality healthcare by combining AI technology with clinical expertise, making primary care accessible 24/7 through health system partnerships. [Accessed 2026-01-02](https://khealth.com/)

### Company Description

K Health is a clinical AI-powered healthcare company that provides virtual primary care services through a mobile app and enterprise health system partnerships. The company's platform uses artificial intelligence trained on billions of clinical data points to help patients understand their symptoms and connect with licensed physicians for diagnosis and treatment.

The company started with a collaboration with Maccabi Healthcare Services, Israel's second-largest HMO, which provided an anonymized database of 2.2 million doctor visits carried out over 25 years [2021-01-19](https://www.calcalistech.com/ctech/articles/0,7340,L-3888606,00.html). This data forms the foundation of K Health's AI symptom checker, which provides "statistical diagnoses" indicating likely causes of ailments based on similar past queries.

K Health operates a B2B2C model, partnering with leading U.S. health systems to provide white-labeled virtual primary care solutions that integrate with existing electronic health records. The company also operates a direct-to-consumer mobile app available in all 50 U.S. states.

## 3. Product & Technology

### Product Overview

K Health offers an AI-powered virtual primary care platform that combines symptom assessment, patient intake automation, and telemedicine services. The platform operates through:

1. **Direct-to-Consumer App**: Mobile app available in all 50 U.S. states providing 24/7 access to AI-powered symptom checking and physician consultations [Accessed 2026-01-02](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/k-health-primary-care/id1180400838)

2. **Health System Partnerships**: White-labeled virtual care platforms for health systems (e.g., Cedars-Sinai Connect, Northwell Primary Care Now, HMH 24/7)

3. **Insurer Integrations**: Partnerships with insurers like Anthem to provide virtual care to members

### Product Portfolio

| Product | Description | Target Segment | Pricing Model |
|---------|-------------|----------------|---------------|
| K Health App | AI symptom checker + telehealth consultations | Consumers (D2C) | Subscription ($9-19/month) or pay-per-visit |
| K for Parents | Virtual pediatrics platform (ages 3-17) | Parents/Families | Included with membership or $9/month |
| Health System Platform | White-labeled 24/7 virtual primary care | Health Systems (B2B) | Enterprise licensing |
| Hydrogen Health | JV with Anthem/Blackstone for employer/insurer market | Employers/Insurers (B2B2C) | Enterprise |
| Weight Management | Medical weight management services | Consumers | Subscription |
| Mental Health | Anxiety and depression treatment | Consumers | Subscription |

### Core Capabilities

- **AI Symptom Checker**: Analyzes patient symptoms against billions of anonymized clinical data points to suggest likely diagnoses [Accessed 2026-01-02](https://khealth.com/)
- **Clinical AI Co-Pilot**: Automates patient intake, presents clinical insights to physicians to optimize diagnosis and treatment decisions [2024-07-01](https://www.fiercehealthcare.com/health-tech/digital-primary-care-startup-k-health-raises-50m-equity-funding-round)
- **24/7 Access Model**: Continuous availability for urgent and primary care needs
- **EHR Integration**: Seamless integration with health system electronic health records (Epic, MyChart) for care continuity [Accessed 2026-01-02](https://leadiq.com/c/k-health/5a1d9dd82300005b008dbf8a)
- **Medical Chat**: Text-based consultations with licensed physicians
- **Prescription Management**: Ability to prescribe medications and order prescriptions through mail-order pharmacy
