## Executive Summary

Innovaccer is a San Francisco-based healthcare technology company specializing in healthcare data activation, analytics, and AI-powered intelligence platforms. Founded in 2014 by Abhinav Shashank, Sandeep Gupta, and Kanav Hasija, the company has grown to become a leading provider of healthcare data platforms for providers, payers, and public sector organizations. Innovaccer achieved unicorn status in February 2021 and has since grown to a $3.45 billion valuation following its January 2025 Series F round. The company has unified over 54 million patient records and works with over 130 large health systems across the United States.

### Company Description

The company's core technology, the Data Activation Platform, integrates data from labs, pharmacy, claims, EMRs, social determinants of health, consumer data, and third-party sources to create unified patient records [Accessed 2026-01-03](https://innovaccer.com/data-activation-platform).

### Core Capabilities

The Data Activation Platform processes healthcare data through five key stages [Accessed 2026-01-03](https://innovaccer.com/data-activation-platform):

1. **Integrate**: Raw data received from source systems and stored in a data lake
   - Supports 2,800+ data elements
   - Integration across 16 states

2. **Harmonize**: Data parsed and codified into a simple data structure
   - 6,000+ data quality rules applied
   - Semantic normalization using AI

3. **Unify**: Patient and provider information aggregated into single records
   - 54M+ patient records unified
   - Master Data Management with Enterprise Master Patient Index

4. **Process**: Intelligent algorithms enrich data with insights
   - Quality measures, gaps in care, risk profiles
   - Social vulnerability profiles, engagement data

5. **Analyze**: Data denormalized for analytics and AI
   - 22% reduction in readmission rates achieved
   - 10% improvement in quality gap closure
