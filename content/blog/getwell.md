## Executive Summary

Get Well (formerly GetWellNetwork) is a digital patient engagement company founded in 2000 by Michael O'Neil following his personal experience as a cancer patient. The company provides interactive patient care solutions that engage, educate, and empower patients throughout their healthcare journey. Get Well was acquired by SAIGroup in July 2024 and subsequently merged with RhythmX AI in November 2025 to form GW RhythmX. The company powers more than 10 million annual patient interactions at over 1,000 healthcare organizations, including 15 of 17 Veterans Health Administration VISNs.

### Company Description

Get Well is a global digital health technology company with more than 20 years of experience in patient engagement. The company pioneered the concept of "Interactive Patient Care" (IPC) and developed the first HL7 integration for an interactive patient care system in 2002 [2020-08](https://www.getwellnetwork.com/20years/).

The company's platform uses bedside TVs, mobile devices, and computers to enable patients to view educational content, access health information, interact with care teams, and manage their care journey. The system includes a library of educational videos and content on specific health conditions, medications, tests, and procedures from content providers like Milner-Fenwick, KidsHealth, Blausen Medical, and others [Accessed 2026-01-02](https://tracxn.com/d/companies/getwellnetwork/__tMifBxnFX01Djr_Wv7pe2UaVEzhLNOSwFGovNWfFazs).

Get Well serves more than 10 million patients annually at over 1,000 hospitals and clinical partner sites. Company claims [Accessed 2026-01-02](https://www.getwellnetwork.com/pr/saigroup-acquires-get-well/) their solutions have:
- Decreased readmission rates by over 40%
- Reduced preterm births by 36%
- Resulted in double-digit growth in brand loyalty metrics
- Driven millions of dollars in revenue for clients
