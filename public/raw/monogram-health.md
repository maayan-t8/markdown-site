## Executive Summary

Monogram Health is a value-based specialty healthcare provider founded in 2019 in Nashville, Tennessee, delivering in-home, evidence-based care for patients with chronic kidney disease (CKD), end-stage kidney disease (ESKD), and related polychronic conditions. The company has experienced exceptional organic growth, ranking as the 3rd fastest-growing private company in the U.S. on the 2024 Inc. 5000 list and 7th in 2025. Monogram Health deploys multispecialty clinical teams to patients' homes, offering comprehensive care management, medication therapy management, and social support services through value-based partnerships with major health plans, health systems, and risk-bearing primary care organizations.

### Company Description

Monogram Health is a leading value-based multispecialty provider of in-home, evidence-based care and benefit management services for patients living with polychronic conditions, notably chronic kidney disease (CKD) and end-stage kidney disease (ESKD). The company was founded by a group of seasoned healthcare experts: former health plan CEO Michael Uchrin; world-renowned nephrologist Dr. Raymond Hakim (author of over 200 published research articles); and former U.S. Senate Majority Leader Bill Frist, M.D., a retired cardiac surgeon and recognized leader in healthcare quality improvement. [Accessed 2025-01-02](https://www.monogramhealth.com/about-us/)

The company deploys an innovative in-home care delivery model combining multispecialty clinical teams with technology-driven clinical services. These services include complex case and disease management, utilization management, and medication therapy management. Monogram's approach addresses not only clinical needs but also social determinants of health (SDOH) such as transportation, nutrition, housing, and financial assistance. [Accessed 2025-01-02](https://www.monogramhealth.com/the-monogram-difference/)

Monogram Health serves patients through value-based partnerships with leading health plans and integrated delivery systems across 36+ states, covering all insurance products including Medicare Advantage, commercial, and Medicaid populations. [2025-03-04](https://www.monogramhealth.com/press/monogram-health-and-memorial-hermann-launch-joint-venture/)

### Product Overview

Monogram Health offers a comprehensive suite of in-home clinical care and benefit management services designed to transform care delivery for patients with chronic kidney disease and related polychronic conditions. The company's model combines multispecialty clinical teams with technology-driven managed services. [Accessed 2025-01-02](https://www.monogramhealth.com/the-monogram-difference/)

| Disease Management | Evidence-based protocols for CKD progression management | CKD/ESKD patients | In-home + remote |

## 7. Competitive Context

### Company-Stated Positioning

Monogram Health positions itself as "the nation's leading value-based multispecialty provider of in-home, evidence-based care" for patients with chronic kidney disease and polychronic conditions. [Accessed 2025-01-02](https://www.monogramhealth.com/about-us/)

### Third-Party Competitive Context

**Key Competitors** (per third-party sources):
- **Somatus**: Venture capital-backed kidney care company; described as one of Monogram's primary competitors [Accessed 2025-01-02](https://pitchbook.com/profiles/company/180203-77)
- **Strive Health**: End-to-end kidney care provider; raised $166M in 2023 [2023-05-31](https://techcrunch.com/2023/05/31/strive-health-166m-kidney-care-healthcare/)
- **InterWell Health**: Formed from merger of Fresenius Health Partners, Cricket Health, and InterWell Health in $2.4B deal [2022-03-21](https://www.fiercehealthcare.com/providers/three-kidney-care-providers-fresenius-health-partners-cricket-health-and-interwell-health)
- **Carenostics**: AI-driven kidney care prediction [Accessed 2025-01-02](https://www.cbinsights.com/company/monogram-health/alternatives-competitors)
