## Executive Summary

Aetna Care Management is the care coordination and medical management division of Aetna, a CVS Health company, delivering comprehensive care management services to over 36 million members across commercial, Medicare, and Medicaid lines of business. Following CVS Health's $70 billion acquisition of Aetna in November 2018, Aetna's care management capabilities have been integrated with CVS Health's retail pharmacy network, MinuteClinic locations, and pharmacy benefit management services to create an integrated healthcare delivery model. The division operates the Aetna One care management platform, which uses predictive analytics, personal outreach, and local access through CVS Health assets to improve health outcomes and reduce costs. Aetna also operates ActiveHealth Management as a subsidiary providing clinical decision support and care management technology to external health plans and employers.

### Company Description

Aetna Care Management represents the care coordination, disease management, utilization management, and population health capabilities within Aetna, a CVS Health company. The division serves as a critical component of CVS Health's integrated healthcare model, which combines health insurance, pharmacy benefits management, retail pharmacy, and clinical services to deliver coordinated care [2018-11-28](https://investors.cvshealth.com/news/news-details/2018/CVS-Health-Completes-Acquisition-of-Aetna-Marking-the-Start-of-Transforming-the-Consumer-Health-Experience/default.aspx).

Following the $70 billion acquisition of Aetna by CVS Health in 2018, Aetna's care management services have been enhanced through integration with CVS Health's extensive retail footprint, including over 9,000 retail pharmacy locations and more than 1,000 MinuteClinic walk-in medical clinics [Accessed 2026-01-03](https://www.cvshealth.com/services/health-insurance.html). This integration enables Aetna to offer care management services that leverage pharmacy data, clinical touchpoints, and local community presence to engage members in their health journeys.

The care management division operates across all of Aetna's business lines, including Commercial (employer-sponsored plans), Medicare Advantage, and Medicaid (through Aetna Better Health). Key capabilities include predictive analytics for identifying at-risk members, care coordination programs, chronic disease management, utilization management, and transitional care support [2024-11-14](https://www.cvshealth.com/news/medicare/focusing-on-value-to-deliver-better-care-to-aetna-medicare-advantage-members.html).

## 3. Business Model & CVS Health Integration

### Revenue Model

Aetna operates within CVS Health's Health Care Benefits segment, which generates revenue through [2025-02-12](https://www.cvshealth.com/news/company-news/cvs-health-corporation-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2024-results.html):

- **Premium Revenue**: Monthly premiums from insured medical, dental, and vision plans
- **Administrative Services Only (ASO)**: Fees for administering self-funded employer plans
- **Government Program Revenue**: Capitated payments from Medicare and Medicaid programs
- **Specialty Services**: Behavioral health, workers' compensation administration, and other specialty services

**Financial Performance (2024)**:
- CVS Health Total Revenue: $372.8 billion [2025-02-12](https://www.cvshealth.com/news/company-news/cvs-health-corporation-reports-fourth-quarter-and-full-year-2024-results.html)
