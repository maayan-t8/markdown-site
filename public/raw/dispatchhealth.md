## Executive Summary

DispatchHealth is a leading provider of high-acuity, in-home medical care founded in 2013 in Denver, Colorado. The company delivers same-day urgent care, hospital-at-home services, transitional care, and mobile imaging directly to patients' homes, positioning itself as an alternative to emergency room visits and hospital stays. DispatchHealth has raised over $737 million in total funding and has treated more than 1.2 million patients across 20+ states. In June 2025, the company completed a merger with Medically Home, creating one of the nation's most comprehensive hospital-at-home platforms. The company has experienced notable restructuring in 2024-2025, including layoffs and market consolidation.

### Company Description

DispatchHealth is a technology-enabled healthcare company that delivers comprehensive in-home medical care as an alternative to emergency rooms and hospitals. Founded by Dr. Mark Prather, an emergency medicine physician, and Kevin Riddleberger, PA-C, MBA, the company was born from their decades of experience in direct patient care and their belief that outstanding medicine can be practiced in settings beyond traditional facilities [Accessed 2026-01-02](https://www.dispatchhealth.com/about-us/our-story/).

The company sends emergency medicine and internal medicine trained medical teams directly to patients' homes, equipped with portable diagnostic tools and treatment capabilities similar to those found in emergency rooms. DispatchHealth partners with health systems, payers, employer groups, and value-based care entities to reduce unnecessary ER visits, hospital stays, and readmissions [2023-10-12](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/dispatchhealth-appoints-jennifer-webster-as-president-bolstering-leadership-team-301954302.html).

In March 2025, DispatchHealth announced a merger with Medically Home, another hospital-at-home pioneer, which closed in June 2025. The combined entity operates across 50 major metropolitan areas in partnership with nearly 40 health systems and most major health plans [2025-03-18](https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/dispatchhealth-and-medically-home-to-merge-increasing-access-to-hospital-level-care-at-home-for-americans-302403835.html).

## 3. Product & Technology

### Product Overview

DispatchHealth provides a comprehensive suite of in-home medical services designed to serve as alternatives to emergency room visits and hospital stays. The company employs emergency medicine and hospitalist-trained clinicians who deliver care in patients' homes using portable medical equipment [Accessed 2026-01-02](https://www.dispatchhealth.com).

### Product Portfolio

| Product | Description | Target Segment | Pricing Model |
|---------|-------------|----------------|---------------|
| ER-Alternative Care | Same-day urgent care for non-life-threatening emergencies | All ages, especially seniors | Insurance co-pay ($0-$45) or $1,000 flat rate |
| Hospital-Alternative Care | Hospital-at-home program for acute conditions | High-acuity patients | Insurance-based |
| Transitional Care | Post-discharge follow-up and recovery support | Post-hospital patients | Insurance-based |
| Mobile Imaging | Portable X-ray and diagnostic imaging | All patients | Insurance-based |

### Core Capabilities [Accessed 2026-01-02](https://www.dispatchhealth.com)

**ER-Alternative Care Services**:
- Treatment for serious and chronic conditions
- On-site lab tests and diagnostics
- IV therapy and medication administration
- Minor procedures and wound care
- Available 8 AM - 10 PM, 7 days a week including holidays

**Hospital-Alternative Care**:
- Hospital-level care delivered at home
- Continuous monitoring and daily clinician visits

- Reduces hospital readmissions
