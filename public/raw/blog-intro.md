## Market Observations

### Consolidation Trends
- Major acquisitions by CVS Health (Aetna, Signify Health) and UnitedHealth/Optum (naviHealth)
- Hospital-at-home market consolidation (DispatchHealth + Medically Home merger)
- AI/ambient intelligence rollup strategy (Commure acquiring Athelas, Augmedix, Memora)

### Valuation Adjustments
- Down rounds observed (K Health: $1.4B to $900M)
- Company closures (Patina Health)
- Restructuring at formerly high-flying companies (Biofourmis)

### Growth Sectors
- Value-based kidney care remains well-funded (Somatus, Monogram, Interwell)
- AI-powered clinical capacity expansion (Counsel Health, Commure)
- Home-based care continues strong momentum despite consolidation

### Strategic Investor Presence
- a16z, GV active in AI-enabled clinical capacity (Counsel Health, Patina)
- Health systems as strategic investors (Mayo, Kaiser in Medically Home)
- Large health plans vertical integration continuing (Humana CenterWell, Aetna)
