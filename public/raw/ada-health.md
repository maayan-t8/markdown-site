## Executive Summary

Ada Health is a Berlin-based digital health company founded in 2011 that develops AI-powered symptom assessment and care navigation technology. The company combines medical expertise with artificial intelligence to help users understand their symptoms and navigate to appropriate care. Ada has completed over 34 million symptom assessments worldwide and serves over 14 million users across nearly 150 countries in 11 languages. The company achieved profitability in 2023 with 260% year-over-year revenue growth and has raised approximately $190-222 million in total funding, most recently securing EUR 30 million in debt financing from IPF Partners in June 2023.

### Company Description

Ada Health is a global digital health company that has developed an AI-powered symptom assessment and care navigation platform. Founded by doctors, scientists, and industry pioneers, the company combines deep medical knowledge with advanced artificial intelligence technology to help individuals understand their health symptoms and make informed decisions about seeking care.

The company's core technology serves as a "digital front door" for healthcare, enabling patients to assess their symptoms 24/7 and receive guidance on appropriate next steps. Ada's platform has been trained by doctors over many years and covers over 3,600 medical conditions linked to more than 30,000 ICD-10 codes, including rare diseases, mental health conditions, women's health, and pediatric conditions [2024-06-06](https://about.ada.com/press/240606-ada-health-expands-leadership-team-for-strategic-growth/).

Ada operates both a direct-to-consumer mobile application and enterprise solutions for health systems, insurers, life sciences companies, and governments. The company's AI platform is certified as a Class IIa medical device under the EU Medical Device Regulation (EU-MDR) [2022-12-15](https://about.ada.com/press/221215-ada-health-receives-eu-mdr-certification/), demonstrating its commitment to clinical safety and regulatory compliance.

The company has positioned itself as a solution to critical healthcare challenges including the shortage of primary care providers, extended appointment wait times (averaging 20+ days in the US), and inefficient patient triage processes. Ada claims its symptom assessment technology is 35% more accurate than competing solutions [2024-06-06](https://about.ada.com/press/240606-ada-health-expands-leadership-team-for-strategic-growth/).
